# 📊 Online Retail Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📌 Project Overview
This project presents an **interactive Online Retail Dashboard** built using Power BI.  
It provides insights into sales performance, customer distribution, and product-level analysis.

---

## 🚀 Key Insights
- 💰 Total Revenue: **9.77M**
- 🧾 Total Invoices: **539.39K**
- 👥 Total Customers: **4372**
- 🌍 Global sales distribution across multiple countries

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### 🔹 Revenue Analysis
- Monthly revenue trend showing business growth
- Peak revenue observed in **November (1.46M)**

### 🔹 Product Insights
- Top-performing products by revenue:
  - DOTCOM POSTAGE
  - REGENCY CAKE
  - WHITE HANGING HEART
  - PARTY BUNTING

### 🔹 Customer Analysis
- Majority of customers are from **United Kingdom (~88.95%)**
- Other countries include Germany, France, Spain, Netherlands, etc.

### 🔹 Quantity vs Revenue
- Scatter plot showing relationship between quantity sold and revenue generated

### 🔹 Geographical Insights
- Map visualization of revenue by country
- Strong concentration of sales in Europe
